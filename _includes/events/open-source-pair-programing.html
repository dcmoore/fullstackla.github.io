Pairing Programing

At Fullstack LA, you're never without a coding partner. Like seasoned developers, we embrace the concept of pair programing. Each person is partnered with another person and the pair work together on an open source project of their choosing.

We believe pair programming is essential to the leveling up process at Fullstack LA because it serves as great way to:

  • Meet new people in the meetup group.

  • Share industry knowledge/tools/tips the other person may not know about.

  • And spot potential bugs before they happen -- two heads being better than one.

Pair programming is sort of like going on a road trip. One person, the driver, sits behind the computer and codes whatever instructions are given by the other person (the navigator). Pairs switch roles after a certain amount of time. The process can be messy, but the destination is all that matters. The destination being a fully functioning app/project!
